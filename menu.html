Our amazing menu

The Child is our smaller burger for the smaller human.
It comes with 100% beef, tasty cheddar cheese and our own made ketchup.

The Standard is our regular burger for those who just want it simple.
It comes with 100% beef, tasty cheddar cheese, tomato, pickles, lettuce and our own made ketchup.

The Chili Béarnaise burger is our burger with a little extra spice on top.
It comes with 100% beef, tasty cheddar cheese, local apple smoked bacon, tomato, lettuce and our own made chili béarnaise.

The Egg & Mayo burger is one of our favorites, egg fried in vinegar.
It comes with 100% beef, local eggs, tomato, lettuce and our own made mayonnaise.

The Caramelized burger is our sweet and salty burger of tastiness.
It comes with 100% beef, tasty cheddar cheese, local apple smoked bacon and our own caramelized onion.

The Onion Ring burger is just as tasty as it sounds.
It comes with 100% beef, deep-fried onion rings, tast mozarella cheese and local apple smoked bacon.

The Over the Top burger is the tower with 4 pieces of meat.
It comes with 100% beef, tasty cheddar cheese, local apple smoked bacon, lettuce and our own made chili mayonnaise.
